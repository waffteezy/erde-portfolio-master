EmployaBLE: Breaking Barriers

Job Portal for PWD

The Project

The primary objective of EmployABLE is to create a job portal specifically designed for PWD users, which will allow them to find suitable employment opportunities that align with their preferences and skills. As my university thesis project, EmployABLE was developed with a dedicated team that worked tirelessly from the outset. We began by refining the registration process, implementing a system that enables PWD users to express their job role preferences from the start. This unique feature allows the system to provide personalized recommendations, streamlining the job search process for PWD users.

Training Feature

it will serve as a registration point and a questionnaire for training seminars conducted online to help screen applicants. The questions will cover the checking of the applicants capabilities and utilities that would help in optimizing their training experience such as the availability of technical tools for an online setup. Lastly, the team created a module for the PWD user that will help them monitor their training progress through a dashboard that will display the amount of time they have spent in a seminar, their attendance, accomplishments, and such. These will all be updated by the admin themselves on their end in the system.

Job Board Feature

The system has a feature designed to assist PWDs in finding job openings that match their interests and abilities. The team put a lot of effort into creating a system that is easy to use and enables users to quickly find jobs that fit their preferences. The main objective of the researchers behind the system is to reduce the amount of time it takes for job seekers to find jobs that are best suited for them. The goal is to make the job search process less daunting and more efficient, especially for PWD users who may face additional challenges in finding suitable employment opportunities.
